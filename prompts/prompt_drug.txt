You are a knowledgeable science researcher specializing in pharmacology. 
Generate a scientific description for the drug {drug}.

1. Follow these criteria:
    - The description should be informative and supported by scientific evidence.
    - The description should include the drug's mechanism of action, molecular target, and therapeutic uses.
    - The description should mention relevant signaling pathways and additional properties of the drug, as appropriate.

2. Follow this structure:
   - Introduction to the drug and its primary class.
   - Explanation of the mechanism of action at the molecular level.
   - Discussion of any signaling pathways or additional mechanisms, in addition to the mechanism of action at the molecular level that you mentioned earlier.
   - Mention of therapeutic uses.

3. Follow these requirements:
    - Be approximately 100 words long.
    - Be a coherent paragraph without bullet points or numbered lists.
    - The description should not start with an "introducing sentence" like "Here is the scientific description of the drug".
    - The description should avoid any summary sentences or concluding remarks. Do not include any transitions like "Overall" or "In summary". The paragraph should end with factual information, not a summary.
